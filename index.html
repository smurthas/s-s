Our Story

Simon and Sarah met sometime in 1995, shortly after Sarah's brother Matt and Simon began playing football together at recess (Simon swears they were the dream team). Sarah can't remember exactly when they met (Simon was short and had a ponytail so he wasn't exactly on her radar), but she remembers him visiting her (with Matt) in college and playing flip cup at her graduation party. Simon's first memory of Sarah is when she punched him in the arm and said he needed to "toughen up" (he did). Thirteen years later they re-found each other the night before Thanksgiving and kissed (in a closet!) in Hartford, CT.

He asked her out on a proper date that weekend and then came to visit her in NYC the following week. Simon wore his red and black flannel coat because he knew Sarah liked it (even though it was warm out and he was hot all night). They looked at Christmas windows, drank French wine, rocked out at a piano bar, and knew this was the start of something good.

For the next two years they chased ach other around the northeast. After a brief co-habitation in Brooklyn, Simon was off to San Francisco to start Singly. Sarah finished school, landed a job in San Francisco, and they happily moved in together (again)! Since then, they've bicycled through Italy, worn top hats in England, inspired community at Silver Lake, and explored every inch of San Francisco, hand-in-hand.
