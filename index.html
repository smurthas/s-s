Our Story

Simon and Sarah met sometime in 1995, when Sarah's brother Matt and Simon started playing football at recess (Simon swears they were the dream team) and video games at Pete Hornyack's. Sarah can't remember when they actually met (Simon was short and had a ponytail thus wasn't exactly on her radar), but she remembers him coming to visit her (with Matt) in college, playing flip cup at her graduation party, sitting shotgun and rocking out to Green Day while she dropped him off at an ugly sweater party, and trying to teach him how to dance at Matt's 21st birthday party. Simon's first memory of Sarah is when she punched him in the arm and told him he needed to "toughen up" (he did). If you had told either of them that they'd end up madly in love 13 years later they both would have laughed, but they are excited to have re-found each other the night before Thanksgiving in 2008 when they kissed (in a closet!) in Hartford, CT.

He asked her out on a proper date later that week before she headed back to New York City and then came to visit on what would later be known as their "Flannelversary". Simon wore his red and black flannel jacket because he knew Sarah liked it even though it was warm out and he was hot all night. Sarah found herself nervous that this guy she had known most of her life was coming to visit and she wasn't sure what they would talk about. She planned an adventure through NYC to look at Christmas windows, drink French wine (neither one of them knew what to order), and they ended up doing a lot of chatting.

That chat and the NYC adventure led to a snowy long weekend in Hartford, a visit to the Cloisters, a picnic on a Hartford rooftop, and many other adventures, followed by two years of chasing each other around the Northeast. They finally moved in together in 2010 when Simon started a tech incubator program in New York and Sarah started her master's at Colubmia. While they loved their apartment and the Brooklyn community, Simon founded Singly and was off to start a team in San Francisco. After finishing school, Sarah landed a job in San Francisco as well and they happily moved in together (again)! Since then they've bicycled through Italy, worn top hats in England, inspired community at Silver Lake, and explored every inch of San Francisco hand in hand. They can't wait for their next adventure: marriage!
