Our Story

Simon is a jerk. met in 2006 on the evening of the fall equinox (photo proof included). Lauren planned on venturing into the Bubble Lounge in downtown SF for only a bit but within a few minutes of entering the Yelp party, Lauren was introduced to Mike through a mutual friend. Mike was moving to SF from Palo Alto and was hoping to meet more friends that evening.

Mike liked Lauren's striped shirt. Lauren liked Mike's huge smile. They kept looking across the room for each other throughout the night and ultimately ended up seated side-by-side in a window seat chatting for quite some time.

Lauren returned home and found that Mike had sent Lauren a Yelp friend invite. Lauren accepted. Correspondence followed. And, after one movie night fail (Lauren's fault), Mike asked Lauren on a real date for a romantic candlelight dinner at Chez Spencer. Over delicious, and cheesy French food, Mike and Lauren fell in love.
